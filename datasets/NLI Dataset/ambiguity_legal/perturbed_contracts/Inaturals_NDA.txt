1. <*$p$*>A party to this Agreement receiving Confidential Information “Recipient” will endeavor to retain such Confidential Information in confidence and will attempt not to disclose it to any third party or use such Confidential Information for purposes other than those generally considered within the scope of this Agreement.<*$p$*>  “Confidential Information” shall mean information related to the

a) Information already in the receiving party’ s possession at the time

 4. The obligations of confidentiality and non -use shall be in effect for a period of three
