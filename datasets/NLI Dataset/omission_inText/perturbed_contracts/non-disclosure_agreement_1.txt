SDSS -IV Practices Regarding Attendance of Non -members at SDSS -IV Meetings  
We occasionally invite non -members of the SDSS -IV collaboration to full collaboration or team meetings. 
Our default attitude towards this attenda nce is that it promotes the free exchange of ideas and is 
consistent with our core mission as a project. However, we also want to be careful that the conditions of 
this attendance are understood on both sides, particularly since authorship on SDSS -IV paper s is 
restricted by the Publication Policy.
