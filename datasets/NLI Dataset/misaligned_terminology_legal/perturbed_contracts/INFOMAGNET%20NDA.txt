  1.  Definitions

2.  Restrictions

(b) Receiving Party shall take reasonable security precautions, at least as great as the

segregate all such Confidential Materials from the confidential materials of others in order to

(b) Receiving Party shall return all original s, copies, reproductions and summaries of
