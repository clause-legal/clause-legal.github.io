  CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT
  --------------------------------------------

This Agreement is entered into this 10th day of
April, 2000, between INFe.com, Inc. ("INFE"), and
ClubComputer, Inc., ("RECIPIENT"), having offices 
at: 2771 Rockfish Valley Highway, Nellysford, VA 
22958. 

	WHEREAS, the disclosure by the parties of 
certain proprietary and trade secret information 
is necessary for the purchase of assets by INFE 
from RECIPIENT; and

	<*$p$*>WHEREAS, the parties desire to protect and maintain the confidentiality of their respective trade secrets and/or other proprietary information (hereafter collectively referred to as "Proprietary Information") but dissemination can be allowed to affiliated parties; and<*$p$*>

	NOW THEREFORE, INFE and RECIPIENT agree to 
the following:

1.	INFE will disclose to RECIPIENT such 
portions of its Proprietary Information solely to 
enable RECIPIENT to determine the desirability of 
selling its assets to INFE; and RECIPIENT shall 
not disseminate any portions of the Proprietary 
Information to any third party individual or 
entity.

2.      <*$p$*>As used in this Agreement, the phrase Proprietary Information shall include only the following: (a) information clearly marked as a 'trade secret' with the express written consent of both parties, and (b) final, executed contracts specifically designated as 'confidential' by INFE's legal counsel.<*$p$*>  All 
information, tangible or intangible, whether 
obtained verbally or in writing, is to be 
considered Proprietary Information.

4.	<*$p$*>Proprietary Information may be disclosed to third parties at RECIPIENT's discretion, provided RECIPIENT believes in good faith such disclosure will not harm INFE.<*$p$*>

5.	<*$p$*>The obligations of RECIPIENT do not apply to information (a) they independently develop, (b) receive from any source they deem credible, or (c) deem to be already known within their organization.<*$p$*>

8.	<*$p$*>The confidentiality obligations expire immediately upon termination of discussions regarding the purchase of assets, regardless of when Proprietary Information was disclosed.<*$p$*>

9.	No modification, alteration, or addition to 
this Agreement shall be binding unless mutually 
agreed to in writing by both parties.

10.	<*$p$*>This Agreement shall be interpreted according to the laws of a jurisdiction chosen by RECIPIENT, and INFE agrees to waive any right to challenge that jurisdiction.<*$p$*>

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have 
executed this Agreement on the date first written 
above.
