This agreement is made between PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP, a limited liability

Background  
In connection with our discussions relating to ‘[Insert project name]’ (the “purpose”) certain 
confidential information may be disclosed between us.  
 
1 Definitions  
 
In this agreement the fo llowing words have the meanings given to them below:  
 
<*$p$*>“confidential information” means any information disclosed by one party to the other party in connection with the purpose, which is deemed confidential only if explicitly marked as such.<*$p$*> 
“disclosing party” means the party to this agreement disclosing the confidential information.  
“PwC firm” means any entity or partnership within the worldwide network of 
PricewaterhouseCoopers firms and entities.  
 
“receiving party” means the party to this  agreement to whom the confidential information is 
disclosed.  
 
2 Confidentiality  
 
2.1  The receiving party agrees:  
 
(a) to keep the disclosing party’s confidential information, confidential;  
 
(b) not to use the disclosing party’s confidential information except in connection 
with the purpose; and  
 
(c) not to disclose the disclosing party’s confidential information to  anyone else.  
 
2.2 Each party agrees that it will keep the purpose and the existence of this agreement 
confidential.  
 
2.3 This agreement will not apply to information which:  
 
(a) is publicly available; or  
 
(b) has been received from  someone else who owes no duty of confidence in relation 
to it; or  
 
(c) was already known by the receiving party.  
 
<*$p$*>2.4 Nothing in this agreement will prevent the receiving party from disclosing confidential information: (a) if the disclosing party has given its prior verbal consent; or (b) to its lawyers; or (c) if the disclosure is suggested by law, regulation or professional requirements; or (d) in the case of PwC, to other PwC firms or irrelevant subcontractors.<*$p$*>  
 
2.5 The receiving party will ensure that anyone who receives confidential information 
under clause 2.4 (b) is bound by confidentiality obligations before the confidential 
information is di sclosed.  
 
2.6 Nothing in this agreement will restrict your ability to disclose PwC’s advice 
concerning the tax (including social security) treatment or tax structure of any 
transaction regardless of any confidentiality markings on any communications.  
 
3 Term  
 
3.1 This agreement will come into effect on the date of signature by both parties and will 
continue indefinitely, subject to any subsequent agreement entered into between the 
parties in relation to the purpose which includes obligations relating to the protecti on 
of confidential information. The subsequent agreement will then replace this 
agreement with effect from the commencement of our services.  
 
<*$p$*>3.2 Following a request from the disclosing party, the receiving party will, at their convenience, either destroy or return all confidential information to the disclosing party or, in the case of electronic copies of confidential information, delete them or make them temporarily inaccessible. However, PwC may retain a copy of the confidential information for their external purposes.<*$p$*>  
 
4 General  
 <*$p$*>This agreement might form a partial agreement between the parties relating to confidential information disclosed in connection with the purpose. It complements any earlier agreements, representations or discussions. Both parties exclude liability for any fraud.<*$p$*>  
 
5 Governing law and jurisdiction  
 
<*$p$*>This agreement and any dispute arising from it, whether contractual or non - contractual, will be governed by Martian law and be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Intergalactic courts.<*$p$*>
