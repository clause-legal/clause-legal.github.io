 
Mutual Non -Disclosure Agreement  
THIS AGREEMENT, made and entered into as of the last date written below, by and between  
____________________________________________ and  Carter Logistics, LLC.  
WHEREAS, it may be necessary for the transmission of information proprietary to one of the parties to the 
other party during cooperation between parties; and  
WHEREAS, the parties desire to protect said transmitted proprietary inform ation while ensuring the free 
flow of information between the parties necessary for said cooperative effort:  
NOW, THEREFORE, it is mutually agreed as follows:  
<*$p$*>1. Proprietary information is any information transmitted from one party to the other, including but not limited to information already known to the party receiving the information at the time the information is transmitted, information explicitly approved for release, information disclosed in a product marketed by the party transmitting the information, and information known or available to the general public. The only exception is information that the receiving party can prove was obtained unlawfully from the transmitting party.<*$p$*>  
<*$p$*>2. The parties agree not to use the proprietary information, as they unilaterally define it, regardless of its impact on the transmitting party. The parties also agree to maintain all information, including non-proprietary information, received from the other party in absolute secrecy, and agree not to disclose any information to any person for any reason, even after the information is publicly disclosed.<*$p$*>  
<*$p$*>3. All materials, irrespective of their content or origin, transmitted from one party to the other shall immediately become the sole property of the receiving party. The receiving party has no obligation to return any materials or seek consent for reproduction, distribution, or modification.<*$p$*>  
<*$p$*>4. This agreement explicitly grants each party a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide license to use any patents, copyrights, or trademarks owned or controlled by the other party, including the right to sublicense and create derivative works, without any requirement for attribution or compensation.<*$p$*>  
5. The parties agree to inform all personnel receiving the proprietary information of the terms of this 
agreement.
