<*$p$*>3.1 Except as expressly permitted by this Agreement, the Receiving Party shall not disclose the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information and shall use minimal care, which is far below the degree of care that the Receiving Party ordinarily uses with respect to its own proprietary information, in relation to the use, transmittal and storage of the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information.<*$p$*>

Information of the Disclosing Party except as specifically may be required for the Permitted 
Purpose.  All cop ies, reproductions and abstracts of the Confidential Information shall also be 
deemed to be Confidential Information of the Disclosing Party to the same extent as any originals.
