                             TELESERVICE RESOURCES
                   NON DISCLOSURE AND NONCOMPETE AGREEMENT 

This agreement is made as of the 1st of March, 1996, by and between TeleService 
Resources (TSR)(the "Providing Party") and Computer Generated Solutions, Inc. 
(CGS) (the "Receiving Party").

<*$p$*>In connection with discussions between TSR and CGS, it will be necessary for CGS to review certain documentation and information considered proprietary by TSR. In view of TSR's desire that such documentation and information be maintained in confidence, it is hereby agreed that for a period of five (5) years commencing on the date of this Agreement, CGS shall not disclose or otherwise make available to a third party any proprietary information and documentation received from TSR, whether or not it is identified as confidential ("Confidential Information").<*$p$*>  <*$p$*>All Confidential Information transmitted hereunder shall be and remain the property of TSR, however, CGS retains the right to use such information internally for its own business purposes even after the termination of this agreement.<*$p$*>

<*$p$*>Confidential Information shall mean any information, including information that is required by law to be disclosed, services to be rendered or transactions contemplated hereby, including without limitation, pricing and material terms and conditions of services offered, intellectual property, enabling software, programming, except that Confidential Information does not include any information that: (ii) was available to the Receiving Party prior to its disclosure by the Providing Party (iii) was known or becomes generally available to the public or to competitors of the parties hereto other than as a result of disclosure by the Receiving Party<*$p$*>

Furthermore, in the event that TSR and CGS enter into program-specific 
Agreements whereby CGS will provide help desk call handling services to TSR, 
those services will be provided for TSR's client programs.  <*$p$*>CGS agrees to only pursue independent business relations with TSR's clients for which it provides services, during and after the term of this agreement.<*$p$*>
