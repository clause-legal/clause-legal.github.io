information, data and/or know-how related to the so-called “Entec Engine” the rights to which are
owned TecTransfer, Inc. a Nevada Corporation, hereby agrees as follows:
1. <*$p$*>Investor shall keep in confidence and not use the Information for its commercial benefit (except for technical and economic evaluation internal to investor).<*$p$*>
2. <*$p$*>Any obligation of investor as set forth in the preceding paragraph shall apply to information disclosed in writing and designated confidential.<*$p$*> <*$p$*>However, this obligation shall not apply to any information, knowledge, data and/or know-how which: (a) Is or hereinafter becomes a part of the public knowledge through no fault of investor; or (b) investor believes was in its possession prior to the time of disclosure by TTI; or (c) investor believes was received by it from a third party who has not received the same from TTI; or (d) Is independently developed by or for investor by persons not having access to Information hereunder.<*$p$*>
3. <*$p$*>Investor shall encourage its employees and the employees of its affiliates who shall have access to any portion of the Information to consider the confidential and proprietary nature of the Information.<*$p$*>
