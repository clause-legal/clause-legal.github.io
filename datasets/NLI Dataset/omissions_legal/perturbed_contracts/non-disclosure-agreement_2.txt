8. RETURN OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION:
All Confidential Information delivered by either Party to the other pursuant to this Agreement shall be and remain the 
property of the Disclosing Party.  No copies shall be made without prior written consent of the Disclosing Party.  All 
Confidential Information including the copies made thereof is the sole property of the Disclosing Party and shall be 
promptly returned to the Disclosing Party or destroyed upon written request by the Disclosing Party.  Nothwithstanding 
the foregoing, a Receiving Party may retain a single archival copy of the received Confidential Information which may be 
used solely for legal evidentiary purposes in the event of a dispute arising under this Agreement.
