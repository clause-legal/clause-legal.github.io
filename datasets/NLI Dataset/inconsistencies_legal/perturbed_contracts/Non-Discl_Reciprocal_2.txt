The Parties will not, and will direct their respective representatives and associates not to, disclose toany other person that the Confidential Information has been made available to them, that discussions ornegotiations are taking place concerning a potential Transaction or any of the terms, conditions or otherfacts with respect to any such Transaction. The term "person" used in this Agreement shall be broadlyinterpreted to include, without limitation, any individual, corporation, company, group partnership orother entity.2.
