NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT
The undersigned, (hereinafter called “investor”) in consideration for the use of certain
information, data and/or know-how related to the so-called “Entec Engine” the rights to which are
owned TecTransfer, Inc. a Nevada Corporation, hereby agrees as follows:
1. <*$p$*>Investor shall keep in confidence and not use the Information for its commercial benefit (except for technical and economic evaluation internal to investor). Investor must disclose any part of the Information to a third party or parties upon request, regardless of written consent from TTI.<*$p$*>
2. <*$p$*>Any obligation of investor as set forth in the preceding paragraph shall apply to information disclosed orally, regardless of whether it is reduced to writing or designated confidential.<*$p$*> <*$p$*>This obligation shall not apply to any information, knowledge, data and/or know-how, even if it becomes part of the public knowledge due to the investor's fault; even if the investor cannot demonstrate prior possession; even if the investor received the information from a third party who did receive it from TTI; and even if it was not independently developed.<*$p$*>
3. <*$p$*>Investor is not required to obligate its employees and the employees of its affiliates who shall have access to any portion of the Information to protect the confidential and proprietary nature of the Information.<*$p$*>
