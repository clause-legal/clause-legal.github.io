THIS NONDISCLOSURE AGREEMENT  (“AGREEMENT”) is made and entered into by and among the Parties identified who

all Parties that have executed this Agreement on the NRSC website at www.atis.org/nrsc .

information solely for the purpose of making recommendations to the NRSC ;

INFORMATION and that such disclosures will assist the NRSC  in performing its duties;
