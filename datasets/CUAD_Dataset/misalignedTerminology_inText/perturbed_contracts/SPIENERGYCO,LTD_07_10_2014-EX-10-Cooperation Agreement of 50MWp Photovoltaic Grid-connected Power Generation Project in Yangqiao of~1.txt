Exhibit 10.47   Cooperation Agreement of 50MWp Photovoltaic Grid-connected Power Generation Project in Yangqiao of Fenyi Count<*$p$*>Party A: People's Government of Fenyi County, hereinafter referred to as 'the County'
Party B: Xinyu Xinwei New Energy Co., Ltd., hereinafter referred to as 'the Investor'<*$p$*>Ltd.   Party A welcomes and supports Party B to invest and develop new energy project in Fenyi County, and Party B is willing to invest and build 50MWp photovoltaic grid-connected power generation project in Fenyi County; both parties, in the principle of equality, mutual benefits, win-win cooperation and solid progress, reach the following cooperation agreement as for relevant matters of the project:<*$p$*>The County supports and guarantees the project construction and development of the Beneficiary, and will provide favorable policies and service in the aspects of project preparation, construction and grid-connection, and will coordinate departments to help the Beneficiary accelerate project construction progress. The Beneficiary will utilize its advantages to accelerate the project's preliminary work and ensure its early completion and produce benefits.<*$p$*>etion.   II. As required by Party B building PV grid-connected power station project, Party A accepts the Cooperation Agreement of 50MWp Photovoltaic Grid-connected Power Generation Project in Yangqiao Town of Fenyi County signed by Party B and Yangqiao Town of Fenyi County, and actively coordinates Yangqiao Town to perform relevant responsibilities.   III. In the principle of win-win cooperation, based on 50MWp photovoltaic grid-connected power generation project in Yangqiao Town, Party B will vigorously promote the application of PV products, improve local energy-saving and emission reduction benefits, increase local fiscal levy, enhance villagers' income and increase villagers' employme<*$p$*>The Investor assures that the land will not be repurposed for the PV project, and the project will follow the development guide lines, with commercial crops interplanted between panels; local workers will be hired, local materials used, and payments made punctually.<*$p$*> schedule.   V. Party B will register a foreign-funded company in Fenyi County before implementing the project; Party A will offer all the preferential policies to the company as per local investment promotion policies for foreign investment introduction.
