EXHIBIT 10.46

                             WEB HOSTING AGREEMENT

This agreement shall void and nullify any and all previous agreements to this date between Galacticomm and Horst Entertainment Inc.

There shall be no additional fees of any kind paid to Galaticomm, other than those stated within this agreement for software usage and/or bandwidth usage.

<*$p$*>Horst Entertainment agrees to pay Galactcomm $0.01 (one cent) per access up to 400,000 accesses. However, Galacticomm reserves the right to adjust the access fee at its discretion with a 30-day notice. Thereafter payment shall be $0.005 (one-half cent) per access.<*$p$*> Horst Entertainment shall send this amount to Galacticomm by no later than Wednesday for accesses used from the previuos week (Monday thru Sunday).

<*$p$*>Galacticomm will endeavor to provide support personnel to address any technical issues. However, the availability of personnel and response times are not guaranteed, and depend on resource availability. Galacticomm must provide a person(s) to correct any technical problems (Server being down or inaccessible) 24 hours per day, 7 days per week. This person(s) must be available by beeper or telephone.<*$p$*> Horst Entertainment shall provide this same 24 hour service at the broadcast location.

<*$p$*>In the event Galacticomm, Inc. chooses to terminate this agreement, Horst Entertainment Inc. may be granted the right, at Galacticomm's sole discretion, to purchase a license copy of the software. The price and terms of such a license, if granted, will be determined by Galacticomm at the time of termination and may vary. In the event Galacticomm, Inc. chooses to terminate this agreement, Horst Entertainment Inc. will have the right to purchase a license copy of the software in the amount of $15,000.00.<*$p$*>

All parties have read and fully agree to all terms and conditions as set forth in this Web Hosting Agreement. Any disputes arising herein shall be settled in a court in FLorida.
